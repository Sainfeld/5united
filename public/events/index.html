5 United's Festivals of Five

A "Festival of Fives" is a regional gathering of BMW /5 riders. Our rallies are informal and generally have fewer than 100 in attendance. Most riders are on vintage and classic BMWs with a high percentage being the /5 model, although all riders are welcome. We encourage you to gas up that airhead and come to a Festival near you!

If you have a /5-friendly rally that you'd like us to post please contact us.

2010 Rallies

Texas Festival of Fives & Texas Airheads April Fools Campout

April 3-5

Quiet Valley Ranch, (830)257-7474, HWY 16 south of Kerrville, TX. Meals available for a nominal charge at the ranch kitchen and communal ice chest provided. Organized by Dan Greenlee dngreenlee@yahoo.com. 5th annual gathering.

Kentucky Festival of Fives

June 4-6

Walnut Meadow RV Park, (859)986-6180, near Berea, Kentucky on HWY 21 (Exit 76 from I-75). Organized by Kelly Ramsey kellyramsey@windstream.net and Dave Anderson postaldave@yahoo.com . Excellent local roads and plenty of nearby restaurants. $20 fee includes two nights camping and Saturday night hotdog cookout. Free coffee provided each morning

West Virginia Festival of Fives

June 18-20

Princess Snowbird Campground within Yokum's Vacationland, (800)772-8342 or (304)567-2351, near Seneca Rocks, WV. No rally fee, just your camping or cabin cost payable to the campground. Everyone provides for their own meals which are available locally and free coffee will be available each morning. Organized by Jeff Tank jefftank@roadrunes.com 13th annual gathering.

Georgia Festival of Fives

August 6-8

Two Wheels Only Motorcycle Resort (706)747-5151 near Suches, GA. Great local riding and excellent meals at a reasonable price from Two Wheels Only. Organized by Matt McCourtney mattmccourtney@mac.com. 7th annual gathering.
